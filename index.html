Research Interests
  • Dr. Li studies competition & coordination, focusing on digital technologies and business models that exacerbate coordination difficulties among different players. Such coordination issues occur when players' interests diverge due to competition, co-opetition, preference heterogeneity, or information asymmetry. Dr. Li's research seeks to improve individual, organizational, and social welfare via the optimal design of information & coordination strategies.
